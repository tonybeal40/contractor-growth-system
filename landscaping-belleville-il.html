Landscaping cost questions in Belleville

Most landscaping prices depend on how much labor, material, disposal, and site cleanup are involved. A small mulch refresh is not the same as brush removal, bed rebuilds, rock installation, grading, or a full curb-appeal reset.

  • Lower-cost projects: trimming, bed cleanup, small mulch refreshes, seasonal cleanup, and punch-list curb appeal.
  • Mid-range projects: multiple beds, decorative rock, edging, shrub cleanup, hauling, and larger yard resets.
  • Higher-cost projects: drainage, grading, hardscape tie-ins, extensive brush removal, large material quantities, and bundled exterior work.
