Questions homeowners and contractors ask

Short, direct answers keep this page useful and help search engines understand the business model without hiding the important details.

Is Bill's List the same as Angi or Thumbtack?

No. It is a local project-start page owned by All-Pro. The goal is a clearer Metro East path, not a national shared-lead marketplace.

Can a request go to another company?

Yes, in the future, if that company is a participating contractor and the project is a better fit. The homeowner consent and disclosure language should stay clear.

Can contractors apply?

Yes. Local companies can request a free listing review or ask about future paid partner options on the contractor listing page.
