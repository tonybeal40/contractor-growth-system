Landscaping Services in Belleville, IL

At All-Pro Construction & Landscape, landscaping is core to who we are. We've been transforming yards across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, and the entire Metro East for 23 years. Bill Sessions is knowledgeable, responsive, and personally involved in every landscape job — from a simple bed refresh to a full front and back yard transformation.

Landscape Installation

Full landscape installs for front yards, back yards, and commercial properties. We handle the design, plant selection, grading, bed prep, planting, and cleanup.

  • Bed edging and preparation
  • Mulch and river rock installation

Landscape Design

Don't know where to start? Bill will walk your property, listen to what you want, and put together a design that works with your home, your budget, and your yard's conditions.

  • Budget-friendly phased approaches available
