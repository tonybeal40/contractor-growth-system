The open position

Construction Crew Member

Work may vary by project and demonstrated skill. Applicants do not need to perform every trade below, but they should identify the work they can complete safely and independently.

  • Support or lead kitchen, bathroom, shower, flooring, drywall, paint, trim, and finish work.
  • Build or repair decks, porches, fencing, patios, concrete, retaining walls, and outdoor features.
  • Complete landscaping, grading, drainage, cleanup, mulch, rock, mowing, and exterior maintenance.
  • Handle material setup, job-site protection, cleanup, punch lists, and respectful homeowner communication.
  • Follow safe work practices, project instructions, and applicable site, equipment, and permit requirements.
