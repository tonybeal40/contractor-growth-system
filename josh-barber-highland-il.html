Common questions

What Highland-area homeowners should know

What does Josh Barber do for All-Pro?

Josh is an All-Pro project consultant. He helps homeowners explain the project, share photos, and start an estimate request with the All-Pro team.

What projects can I discuss with Josh?

Kitchen and bathroom remodeling, decks, patios, fencing, concrete, landscaping, outdoor projects, repairs, and small jobs are all good starting points.

Where does Josh serve?

This page focuses on Highland and nearby communities within roughly 30 miles. Josh can also take requests elsewhere in Metro East. Availability depends on the project address, scope, and schedule.

Who performs and contracts the work?

Josh is the project consultant. All-Pro Construction & Landscape is the service provider and handles scope review, written estimates, agreements, documentation, and approved work.

What affects the final project cost?

Size, material choices, existing conditions, access, demolition, disposal, permits, engineering, utilities, and trade requirements can all affect a written estimate. Online ranges are planning examples, not quotes.

Can I text photos before scheduling a visit?

Yes. Text project photos to Josh at 618-402-8775 with your name, city, and a short description. Photos help the team understand the request, but an on-site review may still be needed.
