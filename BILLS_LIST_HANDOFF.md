## Legal / Disclosure Posture

Keep the public story aligned with these rules:

- Bill's List is a project-match and referral layer.
- All-Pro may handle jobs directly.
- Some requests may be shared with participating contractors.
- Some participating contractors may pay membership, placement, advertising, or referral fees.
- Do not imply neutral or unpaid rankings if paid routing may exist.
- Do not imply Bill's List is the contracting party unless it is explicitly named in a written agreement.
- Do not let partner contractors operate as "Bill's List" or use the brand like a franchise.
