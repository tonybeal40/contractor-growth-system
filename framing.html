Framing Services in Belleville, IL

All-Pro Construction & Landscape provides structural framing for residential additions, garages, new construction, and remodeling projects across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, and the surrounding Metro East. Every frame we build is plumb, level, and square — because everything else depends on it.

Room Additions & Home Additions

Adding space to your home starts with a solid frame. We work from engineered drawings, pull proper permits, and build the addition to integrate seamlessly with your existing structure.

  • First-floor and second-story additions
  • Sunrooms and enclosed porches
  • Bump-outs and extensions
  • Permit management and inspection coordination

Garage Framing & Construction

Detached garages, attached garages, and garage conversions. We frame them properly — with engineered headers, correct wall heights, and roof framing built for the midwest weather load.

  • Detached and attached garage framing
  • Single, double, and triple-car configurations
  • Proper header sizing and load calculations
  • Roof trusses or stick-framed rafters

New Construction Framing

Full new construction framing packages for custom homes and spec builds. We work with builders, architects, and individual homeowners to get the frame up on schedule and built to spec.

  • Complete wall, floor, and roof framing packages
  • Engineered lumber and LVL installation
  • Truss setting and roof framing
  • Subfloor and decking systems

Structural Repairs & Remodel Framing

Opening up walls, relocating load-bearing walls, repairing damaged framing after water or pest damage — we handle the structural side of remodeling projects with the engineering support required to do it safely.

  • Load-bearing wall removal with proper beam installation
  • Window and door opening enlargement
  • Damaged framing repair and sistering
  • Subfloor repair and replacement
