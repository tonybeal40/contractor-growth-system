# Bill Construction Learning Notes

Notes and observations from working in the field and building the contractor growth system.

---

## Business Observations

- Most homeowners find contractors through Google searches
- Reviews and photos are the most important ranking factors
- Consistent activity on Google Business Profile builds ranking over time
- Word of mouth is powerful but not predictable enough on its own

---

## What Works

- Asking for reviews immediately after job completion
- Uploading before and after photos for every project
- Responding to every Google review
- Having a simple website that loads fast on mobile

---

## Key Lessons

Document every project with photos.

Follow up with customers after the job is done.

One new review per week is better than ten reviews once a year.

Google rewards businesses that stay active.
