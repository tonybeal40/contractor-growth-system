# All-Pro SEO Pro Playbook

Date: 2026-06-28
Site: https://allprometroeastconstruction.com/

## Position

Do not chase "dark web SEO secrets." The useful advantage is not hacked links, stolen tools, fake reviews, or private spam networks. Those tactics are risky, short-lived, and conflict with Google's spam policies.

The real edge for All-Pro is a public, repeatable local demand system:

- Strong service pages for the jobs people already search for.
- City/service pages with real local value, not city-name swaps.
- First-party proof: photos, reviews, completed jobs, before/after detail, owner notes.
- Fast conversion paths: smart estimator, quote form, phone, and small-job routing.
- Clean entity signals: consistent business name, phone, service area, sameAs links, schema, sitemap, and crawlable internal links.

## Source-Backed Rules

Google Business Profile local rankings are primarily based on relevance, distance, and prominence. That means All-Pro needs complete service detail, strong local proof, and a visible reputation footprint.

Google Search guidance continues to reward helpful, reliable, people-first content. Thin programmatic pages are not enough. Every city/service page needs useful information a homeowner can act on.

Google structured data guidance supports using LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, ItemList, reviews, hours, service area, and business details when they match visible page content.

Google's AI search guidance points in the same direction: clear technical structure, unique useful content, and expert-led details that go beyond generic copy.

Google's spam policies are the line: avoid hidden text, link abuse, hacked backlinks, fake reviews, auto-generated low-value pages, and doorway-page behavior.
