Roofing Services in Belleville, IL

All-Pro Construction & Landscape provides full-service roofing to homeowners and commercial property owners across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, and the surrounding Metro East. Whether you need a complete roof replacement, storm damage repair, or a thorough inspection before you list your home — we get it done right.

Roof Replacement

When your roof has reached the end of its life, we handle the full tear-off and replacement. We use quality materials from trusted manufacturers and make sure every layer — decking, underlayment, and shingles — is installed to last.

  • Full tear-off and disposal
  • Architectural shingles and 3-tab options
  • Proper ventilation and ice/water barrier
  • Clean jobsite and nail sweep every day

Storm Damage & Insurance Claims

Hail and wind storms hit Metro East hard. We document damage thoroughly, work directly with your insurance adjuster, and make sure you get the full scope of what's owed before we start the job.

  • Free storm damage inspection
  • Insurance claim documentation support
  • We work with all major carriers
  • No out-of-pocket cost when insurance covers it

Roof Repair

Not every issue requires a full replacement. We repair damaged or missing shingles, fix flashing around chimneys and skylights, and stop leaks before they become major problems.

  • Leak detection and repair
  • Flashing repair and replacement
  • Shingle replacement — any size repair
  • Gutter and fascia work
