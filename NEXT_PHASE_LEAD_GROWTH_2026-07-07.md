## Wild Bill Partner Lead Rules

Wild Bill can look for businesses that publicly show:
- Business name
- Website
- Public phone number
- Public email or contact form
- City or service area
- Weak website, weak SEO, missing tracking, poor review workflow, or poor lead capture

Good targets:
- Small family-owned or independent trades
- Electricians, plumbers, HVAC, roofers, cleaners, junk removal, tree service, painters, flooring, and specialty contractors
- Companies serving Belleville, O'Fallon, Edwardsville, Collinsville, Swansea, Shiloh, Glen Carbon, Maryville, Fairview Heights, Granite City, and Metro East IL

Avoid:
- Private consumer data
- Scraped personal leads
- Robocalls
- Spam texts
- Fake listings
- Fake reviews
- Misleading directory claims
