How the listing path works

Bill's List should grow like a trustworthy local resource, not a fake directory. These rules protect homeowners, All-Pro, and future partner contractors.

1. Free listing review

A contractor can submit business details and proof links. A review is free, but it does not guarantee approval, ranking, leads, or a public listing.

2. Proof before visibility

Each listing should show a real business, real service area, real contact path, review signals, and clear categories before it earns visibility.

3. Paid options later

Paid partner slots, sponsorships, referral fees, or enhanced visibility can be tested later, with homeowner disclosure when payment affects routing.
