# Next Phase 7-Day Lead Action Pack

Date: 2026-07-08

Goal: turn the finished SEO work into local calls, quote requests, reviews, and fresh proof for Google, Bing, AI answer engines, and homeowners.

This phase is not a form-provider switch. Keep the current tested lead path live:

- FormSubmit email
- Tony copy and SMS gateway alerts
- Google Sheet lead log
- `thank-you.html` follow-up path
- `review.html` / `review-request.html` review path

## Daily Non-Negotiables

1. Check Gmail for `New All-Pro Lead`.
2. Check `All-Pro Leads` -> `Leads`.
3. Call or text new leads within 5 minutes when possible.
4. Mark lead status: New, Contacted, Estimate Set, Won, Lost, Spam, Vendor Pitch.
5. Save any project photos, city, service, and customer notes for proof.

## What Not To Do

- Do not create fake reviews.
- Do not create fake locations.
- Do not claim neutral directory rankings for Bill's List.
- Do not scrape private consumer leads.
- Do not switch form providers without a full test.
- Do not publish thin AI pages without real homeowner usefulness.
