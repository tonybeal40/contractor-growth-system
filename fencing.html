All-Pro Construction & Landscape installs wood, vinyl, and chain link fencing for homeowners and commercial properties across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, and the surrounding Metro East. We've been installing fence right for 23 years — posts set plumb, gates that swing true, and cleanup before we leave.

Wood Fence Installation

Privacy fences, picket fences, and custom wood fence designs. Pressure-treated posts set in concrete, properly spaced, and built to handle the Illinois freeze-thaw cycle.

  • Privacy, shadowbox, and picket styles
  • Cedar and pressure-treated lumber options
  • Custom gate builds and hardware
  • Dog-proof and pet-safe bottom options

Vinyl & PVC Fence Installation

Low-maintenance vinyl fencing that looks great for years without painting or staining. Available in privacy, picket, and rail styles.

  • Privacy, semi-privacy, and picket styles
  • White and tan color options
  • Aluminum post reinforcement for strength
  • Matching gates and hardware

Chain Link Fence Installation

Residential and commercial chain link for security, pet containment, and property boundaries. Galvanized and vinyl-coated options available.

  • Residential and commercial grades
  • Galvanized and vinyl-coated (black or green)
  • Single and double drive gates
  • Top rail and tension wire finishing
