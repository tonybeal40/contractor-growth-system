# Client Onboarding

## Business Facts

- legal business name and public brand name
- owner-approved phone, email, and website domain
- real office or service-area setup; never invent an address
- actual services and cities the company will serve
- license, insurance, certification, and years-in-business wording with proof
- approved business description and preferred calls to action

## Proof Assets

- logo source file
- client-owned project photos with city, service, date, and short scope
- permission to publish each customer review
- source URL and date for any third-party rating shown
- at least one unique local note for each city page

## Accounts

Grant access through each platform's manager/OAuth process. Do not send passwords by email, chat, or a client intake form.

- domain/DNS
- hosting or Cloudflare
- Google Business Profile
- Google Analytics and Search Console
- Bing Webmaster Tools and Microsoft Clarity
- Facebook Page
- Nextdoor Business Page
- Yelp Business Page
- form/CRM provider

## Launch Acceptance

The client approves the final facts, claims, photos, form recipients, privacy text, and service area in writing. Store the approval with the project record.
