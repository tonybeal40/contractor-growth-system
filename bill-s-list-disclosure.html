Bill's List Metro East Referral Disclosure

Last updated July 1, 2026

Bill's List Metro East is a homeowner-start, project-match, and referral layer operated alongside All-Pro Construction & Landscape.

How This Site Works

Bill's List Metro East helps homeowners start projects by collecting service, city, budget, urgency, and quote-intent information. Some requests may be handled directly by All-Pro. Some requests may be shared with or referred to participating contractors when the project is a better fit for another provider or when referral lanes are active.

All-Pro First Lane

All-Pro is currently the primary contractor lane for many requests submitted through this website. Bill's List is not presented as a fully neutral or exhaustive directory of every contractor in Metro East Illinois.

Paid Participation

Participating contractors may pay membership, placement, advertising, or referral fees to be part of the Bill's List Metro East network. Those fees may affect visibility, routing, category participation, or which contractor is invited into a lead path.

Independence of Contractors

Unless Bill's List or All-Pro is explicitly named as the contracting party in a separate written agreement, any referred contractor remains an independent business responsible for its own licensing, registration, insurance, permits, work quality, pricing, scheduling, contract paperwork, and legal compliance.

No Neutral Ranking Promise

Bill's List Metro East does not promise that listings, routing, or recommendations are purely neutral, unpaid, or exhaustive. Homeowners should review any contractor they hire and request documentation they consider important before signing a contract.

No Guarantee of Availability or Outcome

Submitting a request does not guarantee contractor availability, project acceptance, pricing, timing, or project outcome. For direct All-Pro estimates, use the free estimate page. To compare the broader model, read the Home Service Guide and Metro East Pro Network page.

Questions

If you have questions about how Bill's List Metro East works, use the contact page or call 618-581-0676. Website use is also governed by the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

This page is intended to make the relationship between Bill's List Metro East, All-Pro, homeowners, and participating contractors more clear. It is not individualized legal advice and should be reviewed again if the network expands, handles payments, or adds formal contractor memberships.
