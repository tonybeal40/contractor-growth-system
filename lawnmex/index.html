Our Story

A Family Business Built on Showing Up and Getting It Done

Jeff and Erika have been running Lawn-Mex Inc. out of Belleville, Illinois for over 25 years. They didn't start with a fleet of trucks and a franchise manual — they started with hard work, real expertise, and a reputation for doing what they say they'll do.

That reputation is why customers keep calling back. It's why the reviews write themselves. And it's why Lawn-Mex is one of the most trusted names in lawn care and landscaping across Metro East Illinois and the greater St. Louis area.

We handle residential, commercial, and industrial properties. We service surrounding communities in both Illinois and Missouri. And we do the work ourselves — no sub-contractors farming out your job to someone who's never seen your yard.
