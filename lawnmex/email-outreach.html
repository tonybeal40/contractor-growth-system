👥 Who to Send To — List Segments

Segment your list before sending. Same message to everyone = lower performance. City + status segmentation = more relevant, more booked.

Active Subscribers

Customers on monthly plan. Send upsells, seasonal add-ons, and referral asks.

Past Customers (0–12 mo)

Had a job completed. Send seasonal reminders, subscription offer, and review ask.

Lapsed (12–24 mo)

Send the reactivation campaign. Soft, no pressure. Just open the door.

Quote Requested / Not Booked

Hot leads who didn't convert. Follow up 3 days later, then 10 days later with seasonal urgency.

By City / ZIP

Belleville contacts → Belleville email → belleville-il.html. O'Fallon → O'Fallon page. Etc.

Commercial / Property Managers

Different tone. Lead with reliability, documentation, consistent billing, and volume.

✅ CAN-SPAM Compliance Checklist

  • Use real From name and sending domain (no fake headers)
  • Subject line must not be deceptive
  • Include Lawn-Mex physical mailing address in every footer
  • Include a visible, working unsubscribe link in every marketing email
  • Process unsubscribes within 10 business days (same day is best)
  • Do not email purchased or scraped lists
  • Set SPF, DKIM, and DMARC on your sending domain
  • Keep complaint rate under 0.3%
  • Never incentivize or gate Google reviews

📅 2x/Month Send Calendar

  • Send #1 (1st of month) — Seasonal action + service offer (weather-driven)
  • Send #2 (15th of month) — Proof + education + soft ask (tips, review, referral)
  • After Quote: Immediate confirmation → Day 3 follow-up → Day 10 seasonal push
  • After Job: 24-hour thank you + review link (never gated)
  • Subscription billing: 3-day heads-up before each billing date
