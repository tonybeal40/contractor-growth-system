Website Review Questions

Are All-Pro website reviews submitted by real customers?

The form requires customers to confirm that the review reflects a genuine All-Pro experience. Reviews are checked for authenticity, privacy, and permission before publication.

Does an All-Pro website review also appear on Google?

No. A review submitted here remains a first-party website review. Google reviews must be submitted separately through a confirmed Google Business Profile review link.

Can a customer leave critical feedback?

Yes. All-Pro asks for honest feedback rather than only positive reviews. Genuine concerns can help the company improve and help homeowners make informed decisions.

What should an All-Pro review include?

Helpful reviews identify the project type, Metro East city, communication experience, workmanship, and finished result without including private personal information.
