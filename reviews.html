The review form asks for private project information so All-Pro can match the submission to a completed job. The badge means All-Pro matched the reviewer to a completed All-Pro project using one or more private business records. An email confirmation by itself is not enough.

A review submitted here may appear on this customer reviews page when the customer gives publication permission. Private email and phone details are never displayed.

Yes. All-Pro asks for honest feedback rather than only positive reviews. Genuine concerns can help the company improve and help homeowners make informed decisions.
