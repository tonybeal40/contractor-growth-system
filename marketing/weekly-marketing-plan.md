# Weekly Marketing Plan

Time required: 3 to 5 hours per week.

---

## Weekly Tasks

### Monday
- Review any new Google or Angi inquiries
- Respond to any new reviews received

### Wednesday
- Upload 2 to 4 project photos to Google Business Profile
- Post a project update or completed job photo

### Friday
- Send Google review request to any customers who completed a job this week
- Check inbound call tracking

---

## Monthly Tasks

- Add one new SEO service page to the website
- Upload photos to Yelp and Angi profiles
- Review business listing accuracy on all platforms
- Collect one written testimonial from a recent customer

---

## Tracking

Track each week:
- Number of inbound calls
- Number of estimate appointments set
- Number of new Google reviews received
- Number of photos uploaded

---

## Goal

Build a consistent online presence that generates 10 to 30 inbound homeowner calls per month without paid advertising.
