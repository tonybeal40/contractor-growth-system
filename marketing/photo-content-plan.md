## Why Photos Matter

Photos dramatically increase homeowner trust.

Google ranks businesses higher when they regularly upload new images.

Before and after photos demonstrate the quality of work clearly.

---

## Photo Tips

- Take photos in good lighting (morning or overcast days work best)
- Capture wide shots and close-up detail shots
- Include the homeowner if they are willing
- Label photos by project type and city for SEO benefit

---

## Monthly Goal

Upload at least 4 to 8 new project photos per month across all platforms.
