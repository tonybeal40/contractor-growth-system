# Google Review Request Script

Use these scripts when asking customers for a Google review after completing a job.

---

## In-Person Script

"I really appreciate your business. If you were happy with the work, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps us grow. I can text you the direct link right now if you have your phone handy."

---

## Text Message Script

Hi [Customer Name], this is Bill from All-Pro Construction & Landscape. Thank you for choosing us for your [project type]. We really appreciate your business. If you were happy with the work, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? Here is the direct link: [Google Review Link]. It only takes a minute and it really helps us out. Thank you!

---

## Follow-Up Text (one week later if no review)

Hi [Customer Name], just wanted to follow up and say thank you again for the [project type] project. If you have a moment, we would really appreciate a Google review. Here is the link again: [Google Review Link]. Thanks so much — Bill at All-Pro.

---

## Tips

- Ask in person at job completion while the customer is still happy
- Send the text within 24 hours of finishing the job
- Always send the direct Google review link to make it easy
- Never pressure customers — a simple friendly ask is enough
- Follow up once if no review within one week
- Respond to every review you receive

---

## Review Goal

Target: 50 to 150 Google reviews over time.

Even one new review per week builds strong Google Maps ranking momentum.
