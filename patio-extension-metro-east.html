Expand Your Patio in Metro East, IL

A patio extension is one of the most cost-effective ways to add outdoor living space to your Metro East home. Rather than starting from scratch, we integrate new square footage directly into your existing patio — matching materials, maintaining proper grade and drainage, and delivering a result that looks like it was always there.

Types of patio extensions we build

  • Concrete patio extensions — matching broom, stamped, or smooth finish
  • Brick paver extensions — sourced to match existing color and size
  • Natural stone extensions — flagstone, bluestone, or limestone additions
  • Concrete-to-paver transitions — add pavers alongside existing concrete
  • Side yard and wraparound patio additions
  • Patio extensions with integrated retaining walls or raised sections

What's included in a patio extension project

  • On-site assessment of existing patio condition and materials
  • Excavation and proper sub-base preparation for the new section
  • Material sourcing and matching to existing surface
  • Connection detail between old and new sections for durability
  • Drainage grading to protect the foundation
  • Cleanup and debris removal

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the extension look seamless with my existing patio?

We source materials that match as closely as possible. For concrete, we can often match the finish and color. For pavers and brick, we'll show you samples before starting. Small differences in weathering are natural and typically unnoticeable after a season.

Do I need a permit to extend my patio?

In most Metro East communities, patios under a certain square footage don't require permits. We'll advise you at the estimate visit based on your specific city and project size.

How long does a patio extension take?

Most patio extensions are complete in 1–3 days depending on the size and material. We'll give you a specific timeline with your written estimate.
