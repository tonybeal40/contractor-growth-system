The Story

As a major winter storm bore down on Metro East in December 2022, KMOV reporter David Amelotti went out to find the crews preparing to keep roads and properties safe. He found Lawn-Mex Inc.

Jeff Villavicencio and his team were already loading pallets of calcium chloride and salt, hooking up seven plows of various sizes, and mapping their routes across Swansea, Fairview Heights, O'Fallon, and Belleville — all before the first flake fell.

"It could be five inches. I heard 12 inches. I've seen 12. I don't know what's going to happen."

— Jeff Villavicencio, Owner · Lawn-Mex Inc. · as quoted by KMOV Channel 4

This was Lawn-Mex's first plow run of the season — and Jeff's crew wasn't nervous. They were ready.

"This is a good run, yes. Every day is a challenge, and we like challenges."
