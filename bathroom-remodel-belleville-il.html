Quick answer: what is a bathroom remodel?

A bathroom remodel updates the space so it works better, looks cleaner, and handles moisture correctly. For Belleville homes, that can mean a walk-in shower, new tile, vanity, flooring, ventilation, fixture replacement, or fixing hidden water damage while the room is open.

When to call us

Bathroom Remodel Belleville IL: Cost, Scope, and Estimate Help

If you searched bathroom remodel Belleville IL, the real question is usually scope. A simple refresh, tub-to-shower conversion, and full bathroom gut-and-rebuild are very different jobs. All-Pro helps Belleville homeowners price the room correctly before tile, fixtures, plumbing, and finish selections turn into guesswork.

  • $5,000 to $10,000 range: usually a focused refresh, repairs, fixture replacement, limited flooring, or punch-list work.
  • $10,000 to $25,000 range: common for stronger remodels with shower work, vanity, flooring, ventilation, tile, and plumbing coordination.
  • $25,000+ range: more likely for full rebuilds, custom tile, layout changes, moisture repair, or higher-end finishes.

Belleville Bathroom Remodel FAQ

What affects bathroom remodel cost in Belleville?

Shower type, waterproofing, tile coverage, plumbing changes, vanity quality, flooring, ventilation, demolition, and hidden moisture damage affect cost. We provide a written estimate after reviewing the room.

Can All-Pro convert a tub to a walk-in shower?

Tub-to-shower conversions are one of the project types we evaluate. The existing plumbing, wall condition, shower size, waterproofing, and finish choices determine the final scope.

What happens if you find a soft bathroom floor?

A soft floor can indicate moisture damage. The affected material should be exposed and assessed before new flooring or tile covers it, and any repair is added to the written scope.
