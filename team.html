Founder & Owner

Bill Sessions

Owner & Lead Contractor · All-Pro Construction & Landscape · Belleville, IL

Bill Sessions started All-Pro Construction & Landscape over 23 years ago with a simple idea: do the work yourself, do it right, and stand behind it. While other contractors grew by adding layers of project managers and subcontractors, Bill kept his model owner-led. That means he personally walks every estimate, oversees every crew day, and does the final walkthrough with every homeowner.

Over more than two decades in Metro East Illinois, Bill has built hundreds of decks, installed thousands of feet of fencing, poured countless concrete slabs, and managed complete home remodels — all while maintaining a 4.7-star rating and reputation for being exactly where he says he'll be, doing exactly what he said he'd do.
