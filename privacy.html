Bill's List Metro East & All-Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Bill's List Metro East and All-Pro Construction & Landscape collect through this website, how it is used, when it may be shared, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you submit, such as your name, phone number, email address, city, and preferred contact method.
  • Project information you submit, such as service type, timing, photos, scope notes, budget range, quote intent, and estimate details.
  • Basic website and form metadata, such as page visited, referring site, campaign tags, and the date and time of submission.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to estimate requests, schedule follow-up, and discuss your project.
  • To route or share project requests with All-Pro or participating contractors when that is necessary to respond to your request or when the project is a better fit elsewhere.
  • To improve the website, forms, lead tracking, and local project-match process.
  • To send optional marketing emails only if you clearly opt in.

Email and Marketing

If you submit a project request, we may contact you about that request by phone, text, or email. If you separately opt in to marketing email, we may send occasional project tips, seasonal reminders, promotions, or service updates. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information as a consumer data commodity. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website and process submissions, such as form delivery, analytics, email, spreadsheet, or CRM tools used for business operations. We may also share project-request information with All-Pro or participating contractors when needed to respond to your request, provide a quote path, or refer the project to a better-fit provider.

Third-Party Services

This site uses third-party services to deliver forms and measure website activity. Those providers may process the information you submit through our forms or collect limited technical data when you use the site.

Data Retention

We keep website submissions and related business records only as long as reasonably necessary for estimating, project follow-up, customer service, marketing records, legal compliance, or internal operations.

Your Choices

  • You can choose not to provide optional information.
  • You can ask us to stop sending marketing email.
  • You can contact us if you want us to update or delete information where legally and operationally appropriate.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we keep, but no website or email system is perfectly secure. Do not send Social Security numbers, banking details, or other highly sensitive information through website forms.

Children

This website is intended for adults seeking construction services for homes or properties. We do not knowingly market to children or intentionally collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have a privacy question about this site or want to update your information, contact Bill's List Metro East / All-Pro Construction & Landscape through our contact page or call 618-581-0676.

This page is a practical small-business privacy policy for current website operations. If you later add a customer portal, financing flow, payments, or more sensitive personal data, this policy should be reviewed and upgraded.
