O'Fallon Estimate Checklist

Turn Your Request Into a Useful Project Record

The details below help All-Pro compare your project to the right prior work and prepare for an on-site estimate.

1. Current condition

Send wide and close photos showing the problem, room, yard, deck, fence, or concrete.

2. Desired result

Describe what must change and what you want to keep.

3. Basic dimensions

Include rough length, width, room size, or fence footage when available.

4. Timing and access

Share your target timing, address or city, gate width, stairs, HOA, and permit questions.

Verify Before You Hire

Confirm the Business and the Project

Check public records, request a written scope, and verify current insurance or applicable licensing for the work.
