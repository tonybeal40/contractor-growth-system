Quick answer: what does a patio contractor do?

A patio contractor prepares the outdoor area, handles base work and drainage, and builds a concrete or paver surface that fits the home. In Belleville, patio success usually comes down to slope, water flow, access, material choice, and tying the space into decks, landscaping, doors, and walkways.

When to call us

Call when you want a new outdoor living area, a failing patio replaced, or a concrete space that drains correctly.

What affects cost

Size, access, tear-out, base preparation, drainage, concrete thickness, pavers, steps, borders, and finish level affect price.

Common problems we fix

Cracked patios, pooling water, muddy access, poor slope, trip edges, old slabs, and outdoor spaces that do not get used.

Typical Belleville Patio Project Flow

The most successful patio jobs start with layout, drainage, and use-case decisions before anyone talks finishes. That keeps the estimate closer to reality and helps avoid redesigning the project halfway through.

  1. Site walk and measurements: We review the yard, drainage, door access, and where the patio needs to connect.
  2. Material and layout direction: We compare concrete, pavers, and feature upgrades based on budget and maintenance goals.
  3. Estimate and scheduling: You get clear next-step guidance before the project is placed on the calendar.
  4. Build and final walkthrough: Bill stays involved through construction and closeout.
