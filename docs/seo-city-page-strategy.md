# SEO City Page Strategy

Local SEO city pages help a contractor rank for service searches in specific towns and cities.

---

## How It Works

Google ranks pages that are clearly relevant to a specific location and service.

A city page is a dedicated page targeting one city and one service.

Examples:

- deck-builder-belleville-il.html
- kitchen-remodel-ofallon-il.html
- bathroom-remodel-fairview-heights-il.html

---

## Page Structure

Each city page should include:

1. Headline with city name and service
2. Short description of the service
3. Why hire a local contractor
4. List of services offered
5. Service area mention
6. Call to action with phone number
