# Lead Generation Site

An optional second website designed purely to capture homeowner leads and convert them into calls or form submissions.

---

## Concept

While the main contractor website builds long-term brand authority, a lead generation site focuses entirely on converting traffic into inquiries.

---

## Structure

- Landing page with strong headline
- Short list of services
- Prominent phone number
- Simple contact form
- Social proof (reviews, photos)

---

## Traffic Sources

- Google organic search (SEO)
- Google Business Profile links
- Referral traffic from partner sites

---

## Conversion Goals

- Phone call clicks
- Contact form submissions
- Direction requests

---

## Difference from Main Site

| Main Contractor Site | Lead Generation Site |
|---|---|
| Brand building | Conversion focused |
| Multiple pages | Single landing page |
| Full service info | Minimal, direct content |
| Long-term SEO | Targeted search terms |

---

## When to Build This

Build the lead generation site after the main contractor website is live and indexed.

Use it to target high-value search terms like:

- contractor near me
- home remodeling Belleville IL
- deck builder O'Fallon IL
