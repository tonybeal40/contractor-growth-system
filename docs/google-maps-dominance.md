# Google Maps Dominance Strategy

Most homeowners search Google when they need work done.

Examples:

deck builder near me  
kitchen remodel near me  
drywall repair Belleville IL

The businesses that appear in the top 3 Google Maps results receive the majority of calls.

This strategy focuses on ranking in those results.

---

## Key Ranking Factors

Google Reviews  
Project Photos  
Service Categories  
Website Connection  
Business Activity

---

## Weekly Tasks

Upload project photos

Request customer reviews

Post project updates

Respond to customer reviews

Add new service pages to website

---

## Review Goal

Target:

50 to 150 Google reviews over time.

Even one review per week creates strong ranking signals.

---

## Photo Strategy

Upload:

Before photos

During construction

After photos

Finished project details

Google favors businesses that regularly upload images.
