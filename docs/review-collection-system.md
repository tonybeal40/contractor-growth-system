# Review Collection System

Google reviews are one of the strongest ranking signals for local search.

This document outlines the system for collecting reviews consistently.

---

## Why Reviews Matter

- Increase Google Maps ranking
- Build trust with homeowners
- Differentiate from competitors
- Generate more inbound calls

---

## Review Goal

Target: 50 to 150 Google reviews over time.

Even one new review per week creates strong momentum.

---

## When to Ask

Ask for a review:

- At job completion while the customer is happy
- Via text message within 24 hours of finishing
- During the follow-up call one week after completion

---

## How to Ask

Direct approach works best.

Example script:

"I really appreciate your business. If you were happy with the work, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps us out. I can send you the direct link."

---

## Review Request Process

1. Finish the job
2. Confirm customer satisfaction
3. Send Google review link via text
4. Follow up once if no review within one week

---

## Responding to Reviews

Always respond to every review, positive or negative.

Responses show professionalism and improve Google ranking signals.
