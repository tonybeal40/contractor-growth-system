Questions

Know what happens before you submit.

Can apprentices and experienced tradespeople apply?

Yes. All-Pro welcomes qualified applicants at different career stages. Pay and responsibilities depend on the actual position, demonstrated skills, experience, reliability, and written offer. Applicants are considered without an age preference.

Does submitting guarantee work or leads?

No. Employee applications, subcontractor profiles, Bill's List reviews, affiliate pages, and marketing discussions are reviewed separately. A submission does not guarantee an interview, offer, project, listing, placement, ranking, referral, or lead volume.

Do I have to buy digital services to work with All-Pro?

No. Website, local search, and growth services are optional and separate. Paying for a digital service does not guarantee a subcontract, employee position, homeowner lead, Bill's List approval, or preferred treatment unless a disclosed written agreement specifically says otherwise.

What happens after I apply?

Bill or Tony reviews the information, checks which lane you selected, and follows up when the current need and your qualifications appear to match. Sensitive documents should only be provided later through an approved secure process.
