# Customer Testimonial Script

Use these scripts to collect written testimonials and project stories from satisfied customers.

---

## Purpose

Testimonials are used for:

- Website social proof
- Google Business Profile posts
- Marketing materials
- Building trust with new homeowners

---

## Questions to Ask

Ask the customer one or two of these questions after a completed project:

1. What type of project did we complete for you?
2. What was the result you were most happy with?
3. Would you recommend us to a friend or neighbor?
4. Is there anything that stood out about working with us?

---

## Short Testimonial Request Script

"Would you be willing to share a quick comment about the project we did for you? It could be just one or two sentences. Something like what we built and whether you were happy with it. I can use it on our website."

---

## Example Testimonials

"They built a beautiful deck for our backyard. Professional work and done on time. Would definitely hire again."

"Remodeled our kitchen and it looks amazing. Great communication throughout the whole project."

"Fixed our drywall quickly and matched the texture perfectly. Very happy with the results."

---

## How to Use Testimonials

- Add to the website contact and home pages
- Post to Google Business Profile
- Share in project update posts
- Include in estimate follow-up emails
