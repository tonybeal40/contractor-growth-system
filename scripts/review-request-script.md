# Review Request Script

Use this script when asking customers for a Google review after completing a job.

---

## Text Message Script

Hi [Customer Name], thank you for choosing us for your [project type] project. We really appreciate your business. If you were happy with the work, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? Here is the direct link: [Google Review Link]. It only takes a minute and it really helps us out. Thank you!

---

## In-Person Script

"I really appreciate your business. If you were happy with the work, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps us out. I can send you the direct link right now if you have your phone handy."

---

## Follow-Up Text (if no review after one week)

Hi [Customer Name], just wanted to follow up and say thank you again for the [project type] project. If you have a moment, we would really appreciate a Google review. Here is the link again: [Google Review Link]. Thanks so much!

---

## Tips

- Ask in person at job completion while the customer is still happy
- Send the text within 24 hours of finishing the job
- Make it easy by sending the direct review link
- Never pressure customers — a simple ask is enough
- Always follow up once if no review within one week
