Patio & Retaining Wall Contractor in Belleville, IL

All-Pro Construction & Landscape builds brick, stone, and concrete patios and retaining walls for homeowners across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, and the surrounding Metro East. We've been building outdoor living spaces and solving drainage and grading problems for 23 years.

Brick & Stone Patios

Natural stone, brick paver, and flagstone patios that look incredible and last for decades when installed properly — with the right base preparation underneath.

  • Brick paver and natural stone options
  • Proper base prep — gravel, sand, and compaction
  • Steps, walls, and borders
  • Drainage built into the design

Concrete Patios

Standard and decorative concrete patios. Stamped, colored, or broom finish — properly poured with expansion joints and the right subgrade prep.

  • Standard broom finish and exposed aggregate
  • Stamped and colored concrete
  • Pool decks and walkways
  • Steps and transitions

Retaining Walls

Grade changes, erosion problems, and sloped yards — retaining walls built with the right materials and proper drainage behind them to hold for years.

  • Block, brick, and natural stone walls
  • Proper batter and base depth
  • Drainage tile and gravel backfill
  • Tiered walls and landscaping integration
