Concrete Driveway Maintenance Tips for Illinois Winters

Maintain your concrete driveway through freeze-thaw cycles with these Metro East tips.

Request Estimate Protect Your Driveway

Quick takeaways

Simple maintenance keeps your concrete driveway resisting cracks and heaving.

  • Seal cracks before November to stop freeze-thaw damage.
  • Use sand instead of salt when possible to protect the surface finish.
  • Schedule joint repairs before spring so water doesn’t pool and freeze at edges.
