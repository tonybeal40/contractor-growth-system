Patio vs Deck: Which Outdoor Living Upgrade Fits Metro East Homes?

Patio or deck? Use Metro East-friendly criteria to decide before you design.

Quick takeaways

Use stacked living zones and material coordination to make the right outdoor upgrade.

  • Patios keep you grounded and are ideal for fire pits, seating, and accessibility.
  • Decks elevate views and can be quicker to install on sloped yards.
  • Combine both when you need zones for dining and lounging, plus coordinated plantings.
