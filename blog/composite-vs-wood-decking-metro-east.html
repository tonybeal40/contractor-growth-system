Composite vs Wood Decking for Metro East Weather

Which decking wins in Metro East: low-maintenance composite or classic wood?

Request Estimate Compare Options

Quick takeaways

Metro East homeowners choose materials that stand up to heat, humidity, and freeze-thaw cycles.

  • Composite resists fading, moisture, and bleach, making it a favorite for weather-exposed patios.
  • Wood still delivers natural warmth and can be more budget-friendly when sealed properly.
  • Match railing, color, and lighting to your home architecture, then plan for the maintenance routine.
