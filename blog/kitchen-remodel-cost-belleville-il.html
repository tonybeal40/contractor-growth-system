Quick answer: What does a kitchen remodel cost in Belleville IL?

Every kitchen is different, but here are the ranges we see most often in Belleville and the surrounding Metro East area:

  • Basic refresh (paint, hardware, fixtures, backsplash): $8,000 – $18,000
  • Mid-range remodel (new cabinets, countertops, flooring, appliances): $18,000 – $40,000
  • Full gut renovation (layout change, new everything): $40,000 – $80,000+

These are real Metro East numbers — not national averages from a website that has never set foot in Belleville. Labor costs here are lower than Chicago or St. Louis, which works in your favor.

What drives kitchen remodel costs up?

The single biggest cost driver is whether you are moving anything. Moving a wall, a sink drain, or an electrical panel adds time, permits, and subcontractor costs fast. Here is what typically adds the most to a budget:

  • Moving plumbing — relocating a sink or adding a pot-filler can add $1,500–$4,000
  • Opening a wall — load-bearing walls require a beam and structural work; $3,000–$8,000 depending on span
  • Upgrading countertops — laminate is $20–$40 per sq ft installed; quartz runs $60–$100+ per sq ft
  • Custom cabinetry — semi-custom is fine for most kitchens; full custom adds $5,000–$15,000 over stock
  • High-end appliances — a $500 range vs. a $3,000 range is a customer choice, not a contractor choice

What keeps costs down without cutting corners?

There are smart ways to stretch a kitchen budget without ending up with something you will regret:

  • Keep the layout — if the sink, stove, and fridge stay in the same spots, you save $3,000–$6,000 right there
  • Reface instead of replace cabinets — if the boxes are solid, new doors and hardware look like a full replacement at half the price
  • Choose quartz over granite — quartz is more consistent, lower maintenance, and often similarly priced locally
  • Phase the project — do cabinets and counters now, flooring and backsplash next season if budget is tight
  • Get a written estimate before buying anything — homeowners who shop materials first often find they bought the wrong sizes or quantities

How long does a kitchen remodel take in Belleville IL?

Timeline depends heavily on scope and whether permits are required:

  • Basic update (no permits needed): 1–2 weeks
  • Mid-range remodel (permits for electrical or plumbing): 3–5 weeks
  • Full gut renovation: 6–10 weeks — permit approval in Belleville typically takes 1–2 weeks on top of the build time

We manage all permits. You do not have to chase the city — that is our job.

Do I need a permit for a kitchen remodel in Belleville IL?

It depends on what is being done. Cosmetic work — new countertops, paint, flooring, cabinet refacing — typically does not require a permit in Belleville. But if you are moving or adding electrical circuits, moving plumbing, or opening walls, a permit is required.

Pulling a permit protects you as the homeowner. It means the work was inspected and meets code — which matters when you sell the house. Any contractor who tells you to skip permits to save money is putting their convenience ahead of your investment.
