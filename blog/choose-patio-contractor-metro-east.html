How to Choose a Metro East Patio Contractor

Quick takeaways

Choosing the right patio contractor means checking permits, materials, and communication before signing.

  • Look for documented experience with French drains, grading, and permiable hardscape plans.
  • Ask for detailed scopes so you compare apples to apples when reviewing estimates.
  • Choose a team that shares material samples, color palettes, and maintenance guidance.
