Remodeling Services in Belleville, IL

All-Pro Construction & Landscape handles residential remodeling projects of every size across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, and the surrounding Metro East. From a single bathroom update to a full home renovation, we bring the same crew, the same standards, and direct owner involvement to every job.

Kitchen Remodeling

Your kitchen is the center of your home. We gut it down to the studs when needed and rebuild it right — cabinets, countertops, flooring, plumbing, electrical, and finish work all coordinated under one roof.

  • Custom and semi-custom cabinetry
  • Countertop installation — granite, quartz, laminate
  • Flooring, backsplash, and lighting
  • Full plumbing and electrical coordination

Bathroom Remodeling

Master baths, hall baths, and half-bath updates. We handle tile, fixtures, vanities, showers, and everything in between — properly waterproofed and built to code.

  • Shower and tub replacement
  • Tile work and waterproofing
  • Vanity, toilet, and fixture updates
  • Full gut remodels and cosmetic refreshes

Basement Finishing

Turn your unfinished basement into usable living space. We handle framing, insulation, drywall, flooring, and all the mechanical work to give you a finished space that adds real value.

  • Framing and insulation
  • Drywall, paint, and flooring
  • Egress windows and code compliance
  • Bars, home theaters, offices, bedrooms

Whole-Home Renovations & Additions

Room additions, second-story additions, and complete whole-home renovations. We manage the entire project — permits, trades, timeline, and quality control.

  • Room and garage additions
  • Permit management and inspections
  • All trades coordinated — no sub-sub-contractor chaos
  • Clear timeline and written scope of work
