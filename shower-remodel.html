Custom Shower Remodels & Walk-In Conversions in Metro East IL

Nothing transforms a bathroom like a walk-in shower. All-Pro Construction & Landscape installs custom walk-in showers, converts old bathtubs to modern shower spaces, and replaces tired builder-grade shower surrounds with custom tile work. Frameless glass, built-in niches, bench seating — we build showers that look and feel high-end without the big-box contractor markup. Every shower we build is waterproofed properly, framed correctly, and tiled with precision.

We handle the entire project — waterproofing, backer board, custom tile, glass door installation, and plumbing fixture hookups. No project is too small. Many homeowners in Metro East start with a shower remodel and come back to us for the full bathroom transformation. With 23 years building in Belleville and across the region, we know how to build showers that hold up — no leaks, no grout failures, no shortcuts on the substrate.

Our Shower Remodel Services Include:

  • Walk-In Shower Conversions (Tub Removal) — Remove your old tub, reframe the opening, waterproof, and build a fully custom tiled walk-in shower in its place.
  • Custom Tile Showers (Floor to Ceiling) — Large-format tile, subway tile, mosaic patterns, and decorative liners installed floor-to-ceiling for a polished, high-end look.
  • Frameless & Semi-Frameless Glass Doors — Sleek frameless or semi-frameless glass enclosures that open up the space and showcase your tile work.
  • Built-In Niche Shelving & Bench Seating — Recessed niche shelves tiled to match your surround, plus integrated bench seating for comfort and accessibility.
  • Curbless / Zero-Entry Showers (ADA Accessible) — Barrier-free shower entries with linear drains, perfect for aging-in-place or wheelchair accessibility.
  • Shower Pan & Waterproofing Systems — Proper mortar bed or foam shower pan installation with waterproof membrane systems that prevent moisture damage for years.
  • Plumbing Fixture Upgrades (Rainfall Heads, Body Jets) — Upgrade to rainfall showerheads, handheld sprays, body jets, and thermostatic valve systems.
  • Shower Surround Replacements — Replace cracked, moldy, or dated acrylic surrounds with custom tile installations that last decades.
