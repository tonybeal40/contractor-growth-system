Deck & Porch Contractor in Belleville, IL

All-Pro Construction & Landscape builds and replaces decks and porches for homeowners across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, Collinsville, and the surrounding Metro East. With 23 years of experience and a 4.7-star rating on HomeAdvisor, we're the deck builder the area trusts.

New Deck Construction

Whether you want a simple ground-level deck or a multi-level structure, we design and build it right — properly footed, framed to code, and finished to last.

  • Pressure-treated lumber and composite decking options
  • Footings poured to frost depth for Illinois winters
  • Railings, stairs, and built-in seating
  • Permit management and inspection coordination

Deck Replacement

Old deck rotted out or unsafe? We tear out the old structure, inspect the existing footings, and build a brand new deck that you won't have to worry about for decades.

  • Full tear-down and disposal of old deck
  • Footing assessment — reuse or replace as needed
  • Match existing or upgrade the design
  • Clean jobsite every day

Deck Repair

Not ready for a full replacement? We repair damaged boards, joists, posts, and railings to extend the life of your existing deck safely.

  • Board replacement and refinishing
  • Post and joist repair
  • Railing repair and replacement
  • Ledger board and flashing repair

Porches & Screened Enclosures

Front porches, back porches, and screened-in rooms that add real living space and curb appeal to your home.

  • Covered and open porch construction
  • Screen rooms and three-season spaces
  • Roof integration and drainage
  • Composite, wood, and low-maintenance material options
