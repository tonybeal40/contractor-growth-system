# All-Pro Email Outreach Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This is the operating policy for Codex, OpenClaw, Wild Bill, Google Apps Script, and any future email provider.

## Sender And Delivery

- Send recurring customer marketing only from Bill's current mailbox: `williamosessionallpro@gmail.com`.
- Use `billsessions@allprometroeastconstruction.com` only after the business mailbox is confirmed, authenticated, and connected.
- BCC Tony at `tonybeal40@gmail.com` on each approved marketing message.
- Send to exactly one customer per message. Do not use a customer CC list, BCC list, mail merge, or multi-recipient batch.
- Never imitate Bill from another mailbox or alter headers to make a message appear to come from him.
- Preserve Bill's written `APPROVED` response and keep the campaign approval gate disabled until that response is received.

## Eligibility

- Recurring marketing requires an explicit `Marketing Opt-In` value recorded in the `Leads` sheet.
- An Angi project request may support a direct response about that specific project, but it does not enroll the homeowner in recurring marketing.
- Do not infer consent from an email address, prior estimate, social-media post, purchased list, or directory record.
- Suppressed, unsubscribed, invalid, test, vendor, and internal addresses are never eligible.

## Timing

- Wait at least 28 full days between marketing emails to the same subscriber.
- The campaign runner sends at most one approved message per execution.
- Send no more than six campaign messages per day and leave at least 90 minutes between individually reviewed sends.
- A person reviews the recipient, original project, subject, and body before sending.
- Bill must approve the campaign policy and message before the campaign approval gate is enabled.
- Stop after an unsubscribe, negative response, bounce, or uncertainty about identity or consent.

## Message Standard

- Use the recipient's verified first name. Service interest may guide internal review but is not stated in the recurring availability message.
- Do not guess names, cities, project details, openings, discounts, or completed work.
- The recurring campaign is a general scheduling-availability note. Do not mention a prior inquiry, unfinished project, or completed job.
- Do not include a review request in the recurring availability campaign.
- Write as a short note from Bill. Avoid exaggerated claims, fake urgency, and generic AI-style filler.
- Include the business name, `618-581-0676`, the valid postal address, and a clear unsubscribe link or `reply REMOVE` instruction.
- Send any website-review request separately and only when All-Pro completed work for that customer.
- Every link and claim must match the live website and current business records.

## Opt-Out And Records

- Process unsubscribe requests immediately and no later than the legal deadline.
- Keep the address on the suppression list so it cannot be imported again accidentally.
- Record the sent time, campaign ID, recipient, message ID, sender, and delivery status.
- Keep customer contact data out of the public Git repository and public workflow artifacts.

## Social And Lead Discovery

- Scan only public content made available through approved APIs, public feeds, or manual review.
- Never bypass a login, scrape private groups, harvest private contact information, or automate unsolicited DMs.
- A public post is an opportunity for human review, not permission for recurring email.
- All replies must identify All-Pro and follow the source community's promotion rules.
