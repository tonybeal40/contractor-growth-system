When to repair vs. replace your fence

We recommend repair when: damage is localized to a few posts or panels, the fence is otherwise in good condition, and the material has several more years of life. We recommend replacement when: more than 30% of the fence is damaged, the posts are uniformly rotting at grade, or the fence is simply at end of life. We'll give you an honest assessment at the estimate visit — not a sales pitch for the most expensive option.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my fence needs repair or replacement?

If damage is limited to a few posts or boards, repair is usually the right call. If the fence is old with widespread rot or storm damage, replacement is often more cost-effective. We'll give you an honest recommendation at your free estimate.

Can you match my existing fence style when adding a gate?

Yes. We build gates to match your existing fence material, height, and board pattern so the addition looks original and intentional.

How quickly can you repair my fence?

Many fence repairs can be scheduled within 1–2 weeks of estimate approval. Emergency repairs (downed fence sections after storms) can often be prioritized — call us at 618-581-0676.
