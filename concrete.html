Concrete Services in Belleville, IL

All-Pro Construction & Landscape pours and finishes concrete for homeowners and commercial properties across Belleville, O'Fallon, Fairview Heights, Edwardsville, and the surrounding Metro East. Every job starts with proper subgrade preparation — because concrete is only as good as what's underneath it.

Driveway Replacement & Installation

Cracked, heaved, or crumbling driveways don't just look bad — they get worse every winter. We remove the old material, prep the base properly, and pour a new driveway that's built to handle Illinois freeze-thaw cycles for years to come.

  • Full tear-out and disposal
  • Proper subbase preparation and grading
  • Expansion joints placed correctly
  • Broom finish, exposed aggregate, or stamped options

Patios & Outdoor Flatwork

A properly poured concrete patio adds usable outdoor living space and lasting value. We handle the design, layout, grading, and pour — from simple slabs to stamped and colored decorative work.

  • Standard and stamped concrete patios
  • Pool decks and walkways
  • Steps and landings
  • Decorative coloring and texture options

Sidewalks & Approaches

Public sidewalk repairs, apron replacements, and private walkways. We work to city specifications where required and handle all the coordination with local authorities when needed.

  • City sidewalk replacement and repair
  • Driveway aprons and approaches
  • ADA-compliant ramps and transitions
  • Commercial and HOA flatwork

Footings & Foundation Work

Garage footings, addition foundations, retaining walls, and structural concrete. Poured to code with proper reinforcement and inspection-ready documentation.

  • Garage and addition footings
  • Retaining walls
  • Concrete block and poured wall work
  • Permit-ready structural pours
