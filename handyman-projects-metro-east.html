Deck Repairs in Metro East

Deck repairs are the most common small job we handle. A rotted board, a wobbly railing, a post that sank — these are safety issues that get worse if you wait. We assess the whole deck when we come out so you don't get surprised later.

Common deck repairs we handle

  • Deck board replacement — rotted, cracked, or warped boards swapped out, pressure-treated or composite
  • Railing repair or replacement — loose balusters, broken top rails, full railing swaps
  • Post and footing issues — sinking, heaving, or rotted posts refooted properly
  • Stair repair — stringers, treads, risers, handrails
  • Ledger board repair — where the deck attaches to the house is the most important structural element
  • Joist and beam repair — structural framing that's been compromised by moisture or insects

Safety check included: When we come out for a repair, we walk the whole deck. If we see something that's a safety issue, we'll tell you — honestly, not as a sales pitch. You decide what to do with the information.

Fence Repairs in Metro East

Wind storms, aging posts, and frost heave take down sections of fence every year in Metro East. We handle fence repairs quickly — usually within a week of estimate for smaller jobs.

What we fix

  • Blown or leaning sections — usually a post issue, we dig and reset properly
  • Broken or rotted boards/pickets — wood privacy fence board replacement
  • Gate issues — sagging gates, broken hardware, gate post repair
  • Chain link repairs — fabric, tension bars, posts
  • Vinyl fence repairs — cracked or broken panels and posts
