Bathroom remodels are the #1 most requested home improvement in Metro East. Whether you want a quick refresh or a complete gut remodel, we bring the same attention to detail to a bathroom project as we do to a full addition. You get a written estimate, clear communication, and a finished product you're proud of. With 23 years building in Belleville and surrounding communities, we know what works — and what to avoid — in Metro East homes.

Our Bathroom Remodel Services Include:

  • Full Bathroom Gut Remodels — Complete demo and rebuild from subfloor up, including all plumbing, tile, fixtures, and trim work.
  • Walk-In Shower Conversions — Replace your old tub with a modern, custom-tiled walk-in shower complete with glass door and built-in shelving.
  • Tub-to-Shower Conversions — Maximize your bathroom's usability by removing an unused tub and installing a spacious walk-in shower.
  • Custom Tile Work (Floor & Walls) — Large-format floor tile, subway tile surrounds, mosaic accents, and heated floor systems installed with precision.
  • Vanity & Fixture Replacement — New vanity cabinets, countertops, sinks, faucets, and mirrors — coordinated selections or client-supplied materials.
  • Lighting & Exhaust Fan Upgrades — Proper ventilation and layered lighting that improves both function and resale value.
  • Accessibility Modifications (Grab Bars, Walk-In Tubs) — Safe, code-compliant modifications that allow aging-in-place without sacrificing style.
  • Half Bath Additions — Add a powder room to unused space — closet conversions, basement half baths, and under-stair powder rooms.
