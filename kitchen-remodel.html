Kitchen Remodeling Done Right in Metro East IL

Your kitchen is the heart of your home — and a well-done remodel pays back in daily enjoyment and home value. All-Pro Construction & Landscape handles kitchen remodels from layout planning through cabinet installation, countertops, backsplash tile, lighting, and finish work. Bill Sessions manages every detail. From pulling permits to coordinating plumbing and electrical trades, we run your project so you don't have to.

We work with homeowners across Metro East to design kitchens that fit how they actually cook and live. Open-concept conversions, island additions, full gut remodels, or a focused update to cabinets and countertops — we do it right with a written estimate and clear timeline. No bait-and-switch pricing, no disappearing subcontractors. All-Pro has been the go-to kitchen contractor in Belleville and surrounding Metro East communities since 2003.

Our Kitchen Remodel Services Include:

  • Full Kitchen Gut Remodels — Complete demo and rebuild including layout changes, all new cabinetry, countertops, flooring, and updated mechanical systems.
  • Cabinet Installation & Custom Built-Ins — Semi-custom and custom cabinet installations, crown molding, under-cabinet lighting, and storage solutions.
  • Countertop Installation (Granite, Quartz, Laminate) — Precision-cut countertop installations in your choice of material, with proper substrate prep and sealing.
  • Backsplash Tile Work — Subway tile, mosaic, large-format, or custom patterns installed with tight grout lines and clean transitions.
  • Open-Concept Wall Removal (with Structural Assessment) — Remove walls between kitchen and living areas safely, with proper beam and header installation as required.
  • Island Additions — Custom kitchen islands with seating, storage, power outlets, and coordinated countertop material.
  • Lighting & Electrical Updates — Recessed lighting layouts, under-cabinet task lighting, pendant fixtures, and GFCI outlet upgrades.
  • Plumbing Fixture Upgrades — New sinks, faucets, disposal installations, and dishwasher hookups coordinated with your remodel timeline.
