# Google Business Profile and Review Workflow

Starting point: June 29, 2026.

## Daily Lead Habit

1. Check `All-Pro Leads` -> `Live Leads 2026-06-29+`.
2. Check Bill's Gmail for FormSubmit leads.
3. Reply to every new lead within 5 minutes when possible.
4. Mark the lead source in the Sheet after contact: website, Nextdoor, Google, Angi, Facebook, referral.

## Weekly GBP Rhythm

Monday:
- Add 3 to 5 real project photos.
- Post one update tied to a real service and city.
- Check services, hours, phone, website, and service area.

Wednesday:
- Ask 2 recent customers for a review.
- Add one before/after or progress photo if available.
- Reply to every new review.

Friday:
- Review which pages got leads.
- Add one internal link from a related page to the best-performing problem page.
- Pick one customer story to turn into a proof section.

## Review Ask Text

Hi, this is Bill with All-Pro. Thanks again for trusting us with your project. If you were happy with the work, would you mind leaving a quick Google review? It helps local homeowners in Belleville and O'Fallon know who they can trust.

If you can, mention the city and the project type, like deck repair, fence repair, cleanup, bathroom, kitchen, or handyman work.
